Welcome to Disclaimer Gallery. WE, Sessa Englund, O.K. Fox, Sarah Wang, Shaina Yang, Zaria Poem, and JinHee Kwak pay $360.80 a month to program this room for The Silent Barn.

We are dedicated to sustaining a gallery run by low income people to showcase low income people and otherly marginalized groups. Low income as in low income, zero assets, zero trust funds, zero fucking beer sponsorships.

We want this gallery to be a vivid expression of the experimental and non-hierarchical nature of the Silent Barn. We want to embrace our role within a larger organism. We are queer woman and nonbinary people of different racial backgrounds and that is who we will be showcasing.

We value representation, accessibility, and fighting dominant power structures within the art world.
