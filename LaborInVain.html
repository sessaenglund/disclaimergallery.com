Disclaimer Gallery is proud to premiere #POWERVHS a 60-minute compilation of predominately black and brown, women/femme video artists curated by multimedia artist-performer-extraordinaire RAFiA. The show will feature an exclusive visual installation by RAFiA. We invite you to come enjoy drinks and laughs for the opening. #POWERVHS consists of works by: Hattie Ball Michelle Marie Charles Angelina Fernandez Reagan Holiday Maria Jose Nandi Loaf Elizabeth Mputu Sondra Perry There will be a limited edition of #POWERVHS available on VHS as well as a more affordable edition on DVD. The film will be viewable in Disclaimer Gallery throughout the month.
