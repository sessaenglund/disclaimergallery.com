ELEMENTS OF TRU3MAGIC

The world is full of magic things, patiently waiting for our senses to grow sharper.
- W.B. Yeats Society of New York

A solo multimeda exhibit featuring new work by Madi Dangerously - a storytelling of the magic of spiritual existence while in physical form - an observation of miracles... through portraits, paintings, short film & poetry.

Madi Dangerously (Mariama Rafetna) is a curator, creative director/photographer, spoken word & vocal artist, writer, and visual artist native to NYC. Her art in all its forms aims to celebrate the simplicities that unite us in this fragile life.
